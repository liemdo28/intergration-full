# Quick Start Guide — Integration System

**Version:** 2.3.0
**Audience:** Operators
**Reading time:** 2 minutes

---

## Step 1 — Open the app

Double-click the application to launch.

---

## Step 2 — Check the Home screen

You will immediately see:

- What is ready
- What needs attention
- What to do next

---

## Step 3 — Follow the suggested next step

The system will guide you to the correct action, such as:

- Downloading missing reports
- Reviewing data
- Syncing to QuickBooks

---

## Step 4 — Use guided workflows

Each task is step-by-step:

1. Select your data
2. Review validation
3. Confirm action
4. Review results

---

## Step 5 — Review results

After each task, you will see:

- What was completed
- Any warnings
- What to do next

---

## Step 6 — If something is blocked

The system will explain:

- What is wrong
- Why it matters
- How to fix it

Follow the on-screen instructions. Each blocker includes a direct link to the correct fix.

---

## Step 7 — Use Recovery Center if needed

If the system is not behaving as expected, open **Recovery Center** from the sidebar.

You can:

- Check system health
- Repair settings
- Export support information to send to your support team

---

**No technical knowledge is required.**

The system is designed to guide you through every step. If you are unsure what to do, check the Home screen — it always shows the recommended next action.
